Hello I’m Kamran Khan I founded The Moon Exports and I run it with a vision to deliver high quality Buffalo Horn Products on time. The team of highly skilled artisan with precision tools manufacture genuine Indian handicrafts.

The Moon Exports is registered business entity, registered at Kot West, Sambhal – 244302, Utter Pradesh, India. It’s recognized by Indian director general of foreign trade and member of Export Promotion Council for Handicrafts.

The Moon Exports established on 12th May 2015. I work closely with skilled artisan to help them craft as you desired. Some of our products are Buffalo Horn Plates, Buffalo Horn Middle Tip Plates, Buffalo Horn Button Blanks, Buffalo Horn Finished Buttons, Stag Imitation Buttons made by bones, Bone Blanks, Horn Toggles, Horn Buckles, Rollers, Scales / Plates for Spectacle frames, Hooves, Horn meals & other Handicrafts.

 

Vision

The vision is to promote Horn Handicrafts directly from artisan to provide real handmade crafts from the artisan who have already spent half of their life crafting buffalo horn - in a way that everyone can buy it weather in small quantities or volume sale by the help of multiple artisans.

Mission

  • To become a company to which companies can order their desired handicrafts (i.e. horn crafts, wooden crafts and resin crafts) with trust and reliability.
  • To promote Indian Handicrafts globally especially Buffalo Horn Crafts.
  • To support #MadeinIndia.
